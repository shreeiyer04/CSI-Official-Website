What is CSI-MemberShip?

The Computer Society of India (CSI) is a premier professional body for IT professionals and enthusiasts in India. Established in 1965, CSI aims to promote the exchange of knowledge and ideas between IT professionals, academia, and students to enhance India's technological landscape. CSI membership is an opportunity for individuals, especially students, to become part of this prestigious community and gain numerous benefits that support their professional growth and learning. CSI membership offers a variety of advantages to students, ranging from access to resources, networking opportunities, and professional development. As a member, you become part of a larger ecosystem where technology and innovation thrive. Here’s a breakdown of what CSI membership entails:

Membership plans and Benefits

1 year

₹350

    Benefits

  • Free Entry to Seminars & Workshops: Complimentary access to all CSI Mumbai and CSISIESGST events.
  • Hackathon Participation: Free entry to exclusive CSISIESGST hackathons.
  • Networking Events: Access to annual tech networking events with professionals..
  • Community Badge: Official CSISIESGST digital badge for profiles and events.

  • Discounted Courses & Certifications: Discounts on technical courses and certifications by CSI.
  • Mentorship Program Access: Get guidance from experts in AI, cybersecurity, and web development.
