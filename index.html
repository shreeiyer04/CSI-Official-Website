ABOUT US

WHAT IS CSI ?

The Computer Society of India has come a long way since its foundation in 1965. The founding members felt the need to organize their activities and also wanted to share their knowledge and exchange ideas about the computer sector which was then newly emerging. Today the CSI takes pride in being the largest and most professionally managed association of and for IT professionals in India. It has 72 chapters across India, 511 student branches, and 100,000 members. The purpose of the Society are, Scientific and Educational directed towards the advancement of the theory and practice of computer science and IT.

WHAT WE DO ?

The CSI Campus Chapter of SIES-GST is a part of the well established network of student branches across the country. The Chapter activities are managed by a committee of elected members. The Chapter annually conducts INNOVATIONS, a National Level Technical Project Competition. Additionally, various workshops, seminars, programming contests, are conducted for the members of the CSI Campus Chapter. The idea is to spread the knowledge, and provide opportunities to as many interested as possible.

INNOVATIONS 2K23 RECAP

2K23 EVENT

INNOVATIONS is a national level project competition conducted annually by the CSI Campus Chapter of SIES Graduate School of Technology. It serves as a platform for young minds to take this window of opportunity and bring forward their innovative ideas in the form of projects. Each year teams from various institutes all over Maharashtra as well as Other States, participate in Innovations at large numbers.Innovations 2019 achieved a milestone when it received over a 100 abstract submissions, a feat never seen before.The projects were from various fields such as Computers, Information Technology, Electronics, Internet of Things, Robotics, Mechanical, etc. Out of the received abstracts,45 projects were selected for the competition after carefully analysing each project based on parameters such as novelty, effectiveness, positive impact on society and scalability.

CSI Outreach program

Outreach program

CSI SIES GST conducted a comprehensive outreach program at SIES School, Matunga, enlightening students about the significance of online presence and cyber security. Members from CSI SIES GST engaged students with informative sessions, emphasizing the importance of managing their digital footprint, while also educating them on the various online threats and practical protection strategies. Interactive workshops and open discussions empowered students with essential knowledge and skills to navigate the online world safely and responsibly, leaving a lasting impact on their digital awareness.
